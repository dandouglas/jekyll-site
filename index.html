Hi Daniel Douglas here. I'm a web developer with 6 years experience who has a wide skill set including both design and development. I specialise in Angular, JavaScript, TypeScript, Sass with a sprinkling of C#. My favorite tools for the job are Visual Code and Sketch.

Yep, all your good work on the internet will go unnoticed but if you do anything bad you will find out pretty quickly.
